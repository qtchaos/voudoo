The Beginning

It all started with one thought... we need to make something, and quick!
Here we are, after many months of hard work, we have a website and a
great piece of music that you should totally listen to.

What about the name? Well, one of our members woke up with his body aching
everywhere. He thought to himself: "What if someone made a voodoo doll of me?",
a lightbulb appeared above his head: "Our band is going to be named... Voudoo!"
